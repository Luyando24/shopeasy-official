About ShopEasy Group

Founded in 2019, Shopeasy is an online market/shopping platform with a dream to modernize and improve the shopping culture in Zambia and Africa at large. At shopeasy, we connect sellers to buyers and help businesses advertise and deliver goods to their potential customers.!

Due to the rise of technology advancement nearly all industries have been affected both positively and negatively. Shopeasy is focusing on making use of the technology benefits in the business industry, specifically in marketing and sales, product distribution and promotion.!

Despite the positive effects of technology there still exists a big gap in Africa, though we are first targeting Zambia. We are here to make e-commerce a real thing by introducing a friendly and trusted online platform where businesses can sale their products to customers anywhere in the country and to other countries.

In local Markets we have also observed lack of marketing skills among small business owners and we are here to help them advertise. We have also noticed shortage of space in markets for example street vendors are restricted to freely do business but we do not blame the government because it's for a good purpose.

As shopeasy, we ensure that all businesses that sell with us are given online selling space and free advertisement until further changes.
